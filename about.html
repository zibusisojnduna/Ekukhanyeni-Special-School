ABOUT US

Achievements

Ekukhanyeni Special School excel in extra-mural activities and it boasts of many trophies and medals won by the learners who participated in different sport activities, such as choral music, cultural activities, soccer and netball, cricket, table tennis, athletics and so forth, at regional, provincial and national levels. In 1994 the building of the hostel facility started, that was a very big achivement. Phase one of the hostel was completed. Phase two was completed in 2016. The hostel facility accommodates thirty six girls and thirty six boys. But the other two floors are not used due to the insufficient support staff and the lack of furniture and other items. The hostel nutrition facility that is the kitchen and the dinning hall was completed in 2011. The two additional classrooms were sponsored by Operation Jumpstart in 2010. Three therapy rooms, four classrooms, staff room and administrative toilets were completed in 2014, through the DOE funding. This has assisted the school in providing extended floor space in order to enrol more learners. The school is very proud to have good relationship with local ordinary Public Schools. Ekukhanyeni Special School is also proud to forster partnership with Durban Universuty of Technology (DUT)..
